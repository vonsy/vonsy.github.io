For what it’s worth,it’s never to late,or in my case, too early,to be whoever you want to be.
There is no time limit,start whenever you want.
You can change or stay the same.
There are no rules to this thing.
We can make the best or the worst of it.
I hope you make the best of it.
And I hope you see things that startle you.
I hope you feel things you never felt before.
I hope you meet people with a different point of view.
I hope you live a life you’re proud of.
If you find that you’re not,I hope you have the strenght to start all over again.

“一件事情，无论是太早，或者是对于我来说太晚，都不会阻拦你想成为的那个人。
这个过程没有时间的期限，只要你想，
随时都可以开始。要改变或是改变原状都无所谓，
做事本不应该有所束缚，
我们可以办好这些事，却也可以把它办砸。
但我希望你有时可以驻足这个令你感到惊讶的世界，
体会你从未有过的感觉。
我希望你见到其他和你不同的人们，
我希望你能有一个值得自豪的一生。
如果和你想象的生活不一样，我希望你能有勇气——重新启程。”
— The Curious Case of Benjamin Button (2008)
